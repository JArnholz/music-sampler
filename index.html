RED

Photo by Kristen C.

Red is an American Christian rock band from Nashville, TN. The band's current full-time members are lead singer Michael Barnes and twins Anthony and Randy Armstrong, who play guitar and bass respectively. Red's music combines the heavy guitar riffs, pulsating percussion and gruff vocals of hard rock with dramatic and sometimes eerie ochestral strings, resulting in the band's signature sound that has won eight GMA Dove Awards. Having seen success both within the religious music commuinty and the general population, Red's edgy instrumentals prove to be an interesting juxtapostion to their label as a Christian band. Far from "traditional church music," Red's lyrical content nevertheless contains significant religious undertones while dealing with universally applicable topics such as internal struggle and finding oneself.
